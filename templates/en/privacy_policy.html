Privacy Policy – Johans Digital Forge

1. Introduction

At Johans Digital Forge, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

By contacting us or requesting a quote through this website, you agree to the processing of your data as described below.

2. Data we collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Message or quote request content
  • Technical information such as IP address and browser settings (via cookies)

3. Purpose of processing

Your personal data is used to:

  • respond to questions and quote requests,
  • provide information about our services,
  • improve the website’s performance and security.

Your data is never used for any other purpose and is stored only as long as necessary.

4. Legal basis

We process your data based on:

  • Consent – when you submit a form, you agree to our use of your information.
  • Legitimate interest – to respond to inquiries and maintain customer relationships.

5. Data storage and protection

Your information is securely stored and only accessible to authorized personnel (Johans Digital Forge). Data is deleted once it is no longer needed for the purpose it was collected.

6. Cookies

This website uses cookies to enable essential functions and enhance user experience. We also use Google Fonts, which may retrieve font files from servers outside the EU.

By using this website, you consent to the use of cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Sharing of information

We never sell personal data. Information is only shared with technical providers (e.g., hosting or email services) when necessary for website operation. All such providers comply with GDPR.

8. Your rights

  • access the information we hold about you,
  • request correction or deletion,
  • withdraw your consent at any time.

Contact: info@johansdigitalforge.se
